## Suggested first week

1. Pick one real task from your day job and automate a small piece of it.
2. Revisit one lab without looking at the solution, then compare with [labs-solutions/](labs-solutions/).
3. Bookmark [python_quick_reference.md](python_quick_reference.md) and keep [learning_prompts.md](learning_prompts.md) handy when you get stuck.
4. If you work with CSV or numeric data, start [scientific_python_next.md](scientific_python_next.md).

For a longer learning plan, see [whats-next.md](whats-next.md).
